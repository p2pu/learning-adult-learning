The topics we will cover in this course

Values

What are our learning values? We'll learn about how our family values shape the kinds of learning your children experience.

Spaces

Where do we learn? We learn in a lot of places, not just school. Here, we'll think about all the places that children learn, the results may surprise you!

Technology

My kids and technology, help?!?! Learn about the latest research about using technology with your kids.

All together

It takes a village. Learn about connecting learning for your kids across all the places and experiences they have.
